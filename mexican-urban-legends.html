MEXICAN URBAN LEGENDS

Vector Icons - Trinoodle II

Our project explores 12 well-known Mexican urban legendsâ€”stories that, despite their eerie and unsettling nature, have traditionally been told as bedtime tales for children. We found this contrast fascinating and wanted to flip the script by reimagining these myths in a playful style. Instead of relying on traditional horror aesthetics for this subject (which has been done to death), we took inspiration from classic animation and the vibrant colors of Mexican folk art, transforming these mythical figures into bold, stylized cartoon icons. By doing this, we aim to honor their cultural roots while offering a fresh, playful perspective that reflects how these chilling stories continue to be shared and reimagined over time.
