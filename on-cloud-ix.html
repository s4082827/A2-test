ON CLOUD IX

Vector Digital Assets - Trinoodle II

This project features a website for an event called On Cloud IX - a sky-high, zero-gravity themed music festival designed to lift spirits and lighten the load. Tailored for young people seeking emotional recharge, the event blends mood-boosting music, stress-relieving workshops and games, providing moments of mindfulness in a floating world above the clouds. The website captures this dreamy escape with a retro city pop aesthetic, bold colors, manga-inspired panels, and playful motion. Here, music becomes your emotional wings, and every interaction is a breath of fresh air. Through this interactive website, we aim to bring this fantasy festival concept to reality.
