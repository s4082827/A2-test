'BOUT BOBBLY

Every story started somewhere...
Mine started in Hanoi and has brought me to Ho Chi Minh City, where I am currently pursuing my animation career in RMIT University Vietnam.

I’d call myself versatile and adaptable, guided by curiosity and compassion that keep me exploring many new horizons. Inside me are different “characters”, different skills and interests that each bring something unique to the page. These different sides come together to shape the story I’m building as an artist!
